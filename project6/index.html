Welcome to Aviles Roofing

Aviles Roofing is a family-owned roofing company based in Tampa, Florida. Founded by Saul Aviles after years of working alongside his father, the business brings over 25 years of combined experience to homeowners across Tampa, St. Petersburg, Orlando, and Sarasota.

What makes us different? Unlike larger companies that subcontract labor, we do everything in-house. Customers work directly with the owner from start to finish, ensuring honest communication and quality craftsmanship every step of the way.

Our Mission

To protect Florida families with strong, reliable roofs built to last through sunshine, storms, and everything in between.

Why Choose Aviles Roofing?

  • Licensed Florida roofing contractor
  • Specializes in long-lasting metal roofs, especially standing seam systems
  • Family-owned, with a hands-on owner at every job site
  • 15-year workmanship warranty on shingles and lifetime warranty on metal roofs
  • Serving homeowners across a 50-mile radius of Tampa
